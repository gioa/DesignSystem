Onboarding

Onboarding

Why onboarding?

  • Crucial first impression to increase adoption/conversion
  • Help users succeed with your product from the beginning

How

  • Understand your user’s definition of success, and break down barriers to get them there
  • As your product and user base involve, periodically review and adapt your onboarding experience

Onboarding techniques

  • Guided tour:
    • Familiarize users with core features
    • Inform users of new features
    • Avoid killing momentum
  • Email
    • Educate and support
    • Reengage
  • Sample data and examples
